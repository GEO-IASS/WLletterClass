LED Basics

Regardless of what you call them – electronic message centers, digital displays, reader boards or electronic signs – Watchfire LED signs offer unmatched advertising and branded messaging with crisp and vibrant animation, graphics and video content.

Advertising is key to increasing foot traffic for your location and boosting sales, and many businesses are struggling to get results from traditional advertising media like newspaper, radio and television. An LED sign offers the most cost-effective advertising tool for delivering vibrant, eye-catching messages to your customers.
